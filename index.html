Remarkable.js (a prototype)

Demo

Realtime inline commenting for thoughtful online content

Simple yet powerful

Remarkable is a simple inline commenting platform powered by Firebase that allows users to converse about your content in realtime.

Painless setup

Built with the Firebase API, Remarkable is a quick drop-in to add a rich layer of interaction without the hassle of another backend. Just set up a Firebase, and you're set!

Free and open source

Like all good things in life, Remarkable is free. Though Firebase has a few pricing tiers, Remarkable typically fits the usage requirements for the free tier.
